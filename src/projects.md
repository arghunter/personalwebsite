## Notable Projects

<div class="project-card">
<div class="project-header"><h3><a href="https://github.com/arghunter/clEar" target="_blank">clEar</a></h3><a href="/blogs/clear">Writeup</a></div>

Glasses-based beamforming hearing aids using Golomb-spaced microphone arrays and a custom MSMVDR algorithm. 71% STOI increase, 9.9 dB SNR improvement. ISEF Grand Award, CSEF First Place.
</div>

<div class="project-card">
<div class="project-header"><h3><a href="https://github.com/arghunter/Supermic-tt08" target="_blank">Supermic+more</a></h3><a href="/blogs/supermic">Writeup</a></div>

PDM bitstream signal processing ASICs fabricated on the Skywater 130nm CMOS process via Tiny Tapeout 8. Eight chips targeting beamforming and audio DSP for hearing aids.
</div>

<div class="project-card">
<div class="project-header"><h3><a href="https://isef.net/project/ebed041-novel-pdm-operators-for-efficient-beamforming"> PDM Bitstream Operators for Efficient Beamforming</a></h3><span class="subtext">ISEF 2nd Award Internationally</span></div>

Leveraged microphone PDM bitstreams to achieve a 250× runtime improvement in beamforming while lowering power demands. Custom-designed PCB hardware and FPGA-based acoustic camera.
</div>

<div class="project-card">
<div class="project-header"><h3><a href="https://github.com/arghunter/Potentiostat" target="_blank">Potentiostat</a></h3></div>

18-channel high-speed potentiostat for rapid blood tests and chemical detection. Built with the UC Berkeley Nanotechnology Lab. Includes FPGA firmware and C# driver for a 256-channel electrochemical camera.
</div>

<div class="project-card">
<div class="project-header"><h3><a href="https://github.com/arghunter/FlamProtect" target="_blank">FlamProtect</a></h3></div>

Mathematical wildfire models and genetic algorithms to optimize firebreak placement for wildfire containment.
</div>

## ASIC Designs

<div class="project-card">

16-channel PDM beamformer with DDR input and I2S output, fabricated on Tiny Tapeout 8.
</div>

<div class="project-card">
<div class="project-header"><h3><a href="https://github.com/arghunter/Customizable-PDM-Pitch-Filter-ASIC" target="_blank">Programmable PDM Pitch Filter</a></h3></div>

Narrowband notch filter exploiting PDM's high time resolution to remove specific frequencies from an audio stream.
</div>

<div class="project-card">
