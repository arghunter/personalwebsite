## Why?
I've had a personal website since 10th grade. I always wanted to have a personal space to showcase what I work on, however I personally hate website development, so my sites have always been scuffed and buggy. But then, my friend [Liam Hanrahan](https://www.outercloud.dev/) built his own website, and it looked really good. So naturally, I had to improve mine.

I think everyone should have a personal website that is entirely under their control. Its a way to break free from the algorithm and show yourself freely. I'd love to hear about your website when you make one!

## What Did I Use?
This website is directly forked from Liam Hanrahan's website, with a few improvements of my own. I primarily do hardware and low-level software. Web dev is far from my favorite task, so naturally, I asked his permission and based my website off of his.
